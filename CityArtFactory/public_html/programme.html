Programme

  • International Events

    City Art Factory has already kept its foot print in International market. We have organized art exhibition at ‘The Nehru Centre’ in London as we have been invited by the cultural ministry of the High Commission of India there in London.

    To give financial benefits to the artists, the management of our company has decided to hold a series of international art exhibition in different country, where one artist can take part and visit the country for high end exposure under the concept of Art Tourism developed by City Art Factory.

    You can take part in series of our international events going to be held in London which is the capital and most populous city of England and the United Kingdom, Houston - the most populous city in Texas, Unites States of America, Paris - the capital and the most populous city of France, Austria, officially known as the Republic of Austria in Central Europe, Sydney - the largest city of Australia, Rome the capital of Italy, Athens the capital of Greece in southeastern Europe, Prague capital city of the Czech Republic and Switzerland - officially known as the Swiss Confederation, is a federal republic in Europe.

  • Domestic Events

    City Art Factory (CAF) has come up with new and noble ideas of exhibiting fine art at the five star hotel and five star resorts in different cities in India for the talented artists from all over the country even in abroad.

    Moreover it holds art exhibitions at the reputed art gallery like Jehangir Art Gallery in Mumbai, State Gallery of Fine Art in Hyderabad, India Habitat Centre in New Delhi, Indian Council for Cultural Relations in Kolkata and others.

    City art Factory has successfully launched a new concept of ‘Art Tourism’ exclusively for the artists to give immense exposure to their life where one artist can enrich himself / herself by visiting a new place and observing landscape, celestial beauty of nature and music and interact with artist of other culture, religion and different geographic and topographic location.
