Eco Art Tourism

City art Factory has successfully launched a new concept of 'Eco Art Tourism' exclusively for the artists to give immense exposure to their life where one artist can enrich himself / herself by visiting a new place and observing landscape, celestial beauty of nature and music and interact with artist of other culture, religion and different geographic and topographic location. The term 'Eco Art Tourism' has been coined by the founder Mr. Santanu Roy, which has never been used before in the world of Art. As we have decided to hold series of international events in different country, we have blended Eco Art Tourism' in it.

According to Mr. Roy Eco Art Tourism is defined as a part of sustainable development of our society, which is a process to observe, learn and enjoy celestial beauty of nature (Art created by nature, pertaining to the sky or visible heaven or to the universe beyond the earth's atmosphere) and to assimilate local, ethnic art and cultural heritage by an individual or creative individual (artist) to achieve positive impacts directly on their life (physiological & mental health) and work (i.e. Visual or Performing art).

We are going to organise a serties of events on 'Eco Art Tourism' in India and abroad soon.

Come and join us. Experience art world in a different dimension to enrich yourself with the dazzling beauty of art blended with different culture worldwide.
