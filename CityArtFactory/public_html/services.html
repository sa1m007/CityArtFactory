Our Services

The founder Mr. Santanu Roy says, ‘What I feel, marketing plays an important role in an artist’s life, as it helps to build up artist’s brand name, popularity as well as financial life of an artist’ which is crucial enough for the survival in the art market. Simultaneously artists need to do extensive research work to get an synergic effect when blended with our services for a successful carrier’
Based on his thinking, City Art Factory has come up with different new services exclusively for the artists. These services are essential and will definitely meet the requirement of artists of different level and to make their professional carrier easy enough.

Services include

E-Gallery- This service is designed for international marketing to sale artwork at good price so that one artist can get value for his/her work. New dedicated team has been formed and extensive marketing has been started for sale of art.

Eco Art Tourism - City art Factory has successfully launched a new concept of ‘Eco Art Tourism’ exclusively for the artists to give immense exposure to their life where one artist can enrich himself / herself by visiting a new place and observing landscape, celestial beauty of nature and music and interact with artist of other culture, religion and different geographic and topographic location. As we have decided to hold series of international events in different country, we have blended ‘Eco Art Tourism’ in it.

STPA (Specialized Training Programme on Art) – This service is designed for the artists need to know more techniques from professional mentors, Professors of art collages & to rub shoulder with the famous artists of our country to learn techniques.

Membership Programme - This service is designed for the committed artists who believe in long term commitment and loyalty. City Art Factory has already been made arrangements to offer long term benefit for them.

Pancharatna - City Art Factory has come up with this new idea to offer hassle free art exhibition in five metropolitan cities once in a year for the artists.
It covers New Delhi – The Capital of India, Mumbai – The Financial capital of India, Kolkata- The City of Joy, Bangalore - The Silicon Valley or IT capital of India of India and Goa - one of India's richest states.

Art Display - This service is designed for direct sale of art work from the Five Star Hotels & Five Star Resorts all over the country.

Artist Buyer Meet - This service is designed exclusively for the artists to get comments of their works and to interact with high profile buyers across the country even from abroad at five star hotels and five star resorts.
